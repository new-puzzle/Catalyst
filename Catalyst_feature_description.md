# Catalyst - AI Smart Journal

## What is Catalyst?

Catalyst is your personal AI-powered journaling companion. Think of it as a smart diary that not only remembers everything you tell it but also helps you understand yourself better over time.

## Key Features

### Voice-First Experience
- **Talk naturally** - Just speak your thoughts like you're talking to a friend
- **Real-time conversation** - The AI responds with voice, creating a natural back-and-forth dialogue
- **Emotion detection** - Understands not just what you say, but how you feel when saying it

### Four Conversation Modes

1. **Auto Mode** (Daily Journaling)
   - Perfect for everyday thoughts and reflections
   - Quick, affordable responses
   - Best for regular check-ins

2. **Architect Mode** (Planning & Goals)
   - Helps structure your ideas
   - Creates action plans
   - Breaks down complex goals into steps

3. **Simulator Mode** (Practice Conversations)
   - Rehearse job interviews
   - Prepare for difficult conversations
   - Get feedback on communication

4. **Scribe Mode** (Writing Assistant)
   - Transforms rough thoughts into polished text
   - Helps write emails, posts, documents
   - Maintains your personal voice

### Smart Memory (RAG)
- **Remembers your history** - References past conversations naturally
- **Spots patterns** - Notices recurring themes in your life
- **Tracks progress** - Sees how your goals and feelings evolve

### Automatic Insights
- **Daily summaries** - Get a recap of your day at 11 PM
- **Weekly reviews** - Comprehensive analysis every Sunday
- **Emotion trends** - Visualize your emotional patterns
- **Goal tracking** - Monitor progress on what matters to you

### Privacy & Security
- **Google Sign-In** - Secure authentication
- **Your data stays yours** - Local database storage
- **Edit or delete** - Full control over your entries

### Mobile-Friendly
- Works great on phones and tablets
- Install as an app (PWA)
- Works offline for viewing past entries

## How It Works

1. **Sign in** with your Google account
2. **Choose a mode** based on what you need
3. **Start talking or typing** your thoughts
4. **Get AI responses** that understand context
5. **Review insights** from daily/weekly summaries

## Who Is This For?

- People who want to journal but find writing tedious
- Anyone seeking self-improvement and reflection
- Professionals preparing for important conversations
- Writers looking for a brainstorming partner
- Anyone who wants to understand their emotional patterns better

## Cost Efficiency

Catalyst uses different AI models strategically:
- **Gemini Flash** for daily journaling (very affordable)
- **DeepSeek** for planning tasks (cost-effective)
- **Claude** for high-quality writing (premium, used sparingly)

This means you get the right AI for each task without overpaying.
